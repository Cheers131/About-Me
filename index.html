About Me

I never got this question from my fellow readers, and I think I’ll never get this from anyone.

Why the name “Cheers!”?

It is not just a random English word chosen from the dictionary or the most frequently used greeting of the British. It is not something I say before sipping a glass of wine, if I could, or a way to express my happiness to others. Rather, it is something much more than that.

The prospect of writing something that others would read and ponder fascinated me when I started writing a diary. Reading someone’s experience will put you in their shoes, and I really wanted to be that someone. So, I wrote one page per day to get better at writing for about six months. What happened? I got emotionally attached to those six months of my life, and that’s where I found “Cheers!”. It was the staple word of my diary and became my very own thought machine, where you read my heinous thoughts.

And who am I? That’s a question for you to find out.
